🍕 Classic margherita recipe

  • 1 pizza dough base
  • 1/2 cup crushed tomatoes
  • Fresh mozzarella slices
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Olive oil and salt

Spread the tomatoes on the dough, add mozzarella and basil, drizzle olive oil, bake at 220°C for 10-12 minutes. Buon appetito!
